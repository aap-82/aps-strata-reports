How we work

Insight backed by data.

Every Access Property Services report combines two things — Dominic Ogburn's hands-on experience inspecting NSW residential buildings, and a proprietary AI document analysis tool that reads every page of the strata library so nothing slips through. The judgement to see what matters, paired with the thoroughness to never miss a detail.

01 · Experience

Sees what others don't.

Years of NSW residential construction and inspection work — Dominic knows where to look, what to question, and what's structurally meaningful versus cosmetic. Pattern-matching from hundreds of inspections, applied to your property.

02 · AI Analysis

Doesn't miss a page.

Our proprietary AI document analysis tool reads the full strata library — minutes, financials, expert reports, correspondence, invoices. It surfaces inconsistencies, finds patterns, and flags what's missing. Most strata reports stop at 3 years; we analyse every document available — whether that's 5 years of records or 10.
